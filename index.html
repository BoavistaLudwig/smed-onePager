When minutes matter, better use SMED

SMED is one tool from Lean Management and used to reduce the amount of time it takes to change from one process to running another.

Digital

A better way to apply SMED

Thanks to the SMED app you can now apply the SMED methodology to any process, at any time, from your iPhone.

Record Times

Time minutes to hour-long sessions and label any steps you recognize according to the SMED methodology

Templates

Apply any of the existing templates or create your own to save time with labeling while recording times

Analyze

Review your recorded session and externalize those steps you deem external to the ideal process

Reports

Receive detailed reports with multiple metrics for every recorded session (soon these will be shareable)

Dashboard

Keep track of your activity and global metrics in your personal dashboard

Cloud Storage

Your data is saved in the cloud for your personal convenience and for future SMED functionalities
